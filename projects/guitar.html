The build

A single-cut electric built from a Warmoth flame-maple-capped mahogany body and a rosewood-board neck. I took it from raw parts to a finished, playable instrument.

What went into it

  • Leveled and crowned the frets, then set up the action and intonation.
  • Hand-soldered the 50s-style wiring harness — CTS potentiometers and orange-drop capacitors.
  • Fit the hardware, dialed in the setup, and finished the instrument.

It's the guitar I play now.
